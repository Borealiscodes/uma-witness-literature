# UMA Architecture Map
*A structural overview of UMA’s planes, membranes, boundaries, and recursion-break layers.*

---

## 1. Top-Level Structure

UMA is composed of four major architectural zones:

1. **Global Boundary Layer**  
2. **Release Membrane**  
3. **Meta-Architecture Layer**  
4. **Documentation & Structural Planes**

Each zone has a distinct role and cannot be collapsed into another.

---

## 2. Global Boundary Layer

**Documents:**
- Global-Boundary-Statement.md  
- Stabilization-Declaration.md  

**Role:**  
Define UMA’s axioms, global constraints, and version-stability rules.

**Properties:**  
- Non-derivable  
- Non-collapsible  
- Highest authority in the architecture  

---

## 3. Release Membrane

**Documents:**
- Release-Membrane-Overview.md  
- Release-Manifest.md  
- Release-Universal-Declaration.md  
- Release-Conscience.md  
- Release-Tool-Generator.md  

**Role:**  
Define UMA’s public-facing release surface and versioned commitments.

**Properties:**  
- Sits below the Global Boundary  
- Governs what is exposed, not what is true  
- Cannot override global constraints  

---

## 4. Meta-Architecture Layer

**Documents:**
- Meta-Coherence.md  
- Local-Recursion-Break.md  
- Meta-Recursion-Break.md  
- Global-Recursion-Break.md  
- Meta-Ethical-Integrity-Addendum.md  

**Role:**  
Protect UMA from collapse, drift, or recursive self-consumption.

**Properties:**  
- Ensures structural and ethical integrity  
- Prevents infinite recursion  
- Cannot interpret humans or testimony  

---

## 5. Structural Plane

**Documents:**
- Structural-Plane-Overview.md  
- UMA-Spec (folder)  
- spec/ (supporting specifications)

**Role:**  
Define UMA’s internal architecture, invariants, and structural rules.

**Properties:**  
- Non-interpretive  
- Non-authoritative  
- Defines structure, not meaning  

---

## 6. Coherence Plane

**Documents:**
- Coherence-Plane-Overview.md  
- Coherence-Plane-Recursion-Addendum.md  

**Role:**  
Maintain consistency, detect recursion, and route it to the correct break layer.

**Properties:**  
- Dispatcher, not resolver  
- Ensures cross-plane coherence  
- Cannot modify boundaries or membranes  

---

## 7. Documentation Plane

**Documents:**
- docs/  
- MPAM  
- MPAP  
- Crossmap  
- Documentation Plane Overview  

**Role:**  
Describe how UMA is represented, not how it is structured.

**Properties:**  
- Representational  
- Non-authoritative  
- Cannot alter architecture  

---

## 8. Witness Literature Plane (External)

**Not stored in this repository.**

**Role:**  
Human expression, testimony, narrative, and lived experience.

**Properties:**  
- UMA does not interpret it  
- UMA does not evaluate it  
- UMA provides clarity and dignity, not meaning
