# **Release Boundary Layer**  
*A protective membrane between this repository and all external use.*

This repository does not govern, direct, assess, evaluate, or interpret any human being.  
It does not define norms, prescribe behavior, or establish authority over meaning, identity, or experience.  
Nothing within this repository may be used to infer mental states, determine intent, evaluate credibility, or replace human care, legal protections, or therapeutic support.

The documents contained here — including specifications, frameworks, tools, templates, and conceptual structures — are **scaffolds**, not truths; **supports**, not substitutes; **maps**, not mandates.  
They describe architecture, not people.  
They provide structure, not interpretation.  
They accompany, but do not guide.

No component of this repository may be used for governance, assessment, decision‑making, enforcement, or any form of authority over a person or group.  
All tools, frameworks, and specifications must remain optional, non‑coercive, non‑substitutive, and non‑interpretive.

This repository must not be used to diagnose, categorize, classify, or evaluate individuals or communities.  
It must not be used to create systems that collapse human meaning into machine structure or machine structure into human meaning.

All use of this repository must preserve:

- **Meaning Sovereignty**  
- **Boundary Integrity**  
- **Non‑Authority**  
- **Non‑Coercion**  
- **Sādhana Alignment**  

This boundary layer applies universally to all documents, tools, and structures within this repository and must be read as a standing constraint on their interpretation, implementation, and distribution.
