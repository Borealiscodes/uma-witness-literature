Deterministic v0.4 is the first version that moves beyond:

- single‑operator behavior  
- single‑manifold activation  
- single‑envelope stability  

and begins defining:

- **multi‑operator deterministic dynamics**  
- **cross‑manifold deterministic interactions**  
- **temporal deepening beyond v0.3**  
- **multi‑stage activation sequences**  
- **multi‑stage rollback recovery**  

v0.4 is the “multi‑operator era” of deterministic NDH.

# 🧩 **2. Structural Goals for v0.4**

### **2.1 Multi‑Operator Deterministic Behavior**
v0.4 introduces deterministic behavior involving **multiple operators acting simultaneously** inside the invariant region.

This requires:

- shared activation thresholds  
- shared rollback anchors  
- shared pressure gradients  
- shared temporal envelopes  

Operators must coordinate without violating invariants.

---

### **2.2 Temporal Deepening**
v0.3 introduced temporal stability.  
v0.4 introduces **temporal deepening**, meaning:

- operators maintain stability over longer sequences  
- pressure gradients evolve over time  
- rollback remains reachable even after extended activation  
- deterministic behavior becomes “long‑form”  

This is essential for NDH’s long‑duration deterministic chains.

---

### **2.3 Cross‑Manifold Deterministic Integration**
v0.4 expands deterministic behavior to consume:

- **Verdant Deep expressive pressure**  
- **Lean 4 formal invariants**  
- **Emergent case‑study manifolds**  

This is the first version where deterministic behavior becomes **multi‑manifold aware**.
