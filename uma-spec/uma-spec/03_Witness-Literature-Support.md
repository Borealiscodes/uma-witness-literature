# Witness Literature Support Module
*Structured, non‑interpretive support for survivors documenting harm within UMA.*

---

**Cross‑Reference:**  
This module operates under the constraints and invariants defined in 02_Boundary-Layer.md and must not be used independently of the Boundary Layer.

```

# **1. Purpose**  
The Witness Literature Support Module provides **structural, non‑interpretive assistance** to individuals documenting experiences of torture, ill‑treatment, institutional harm, or systemic failure.  
It enables survivors to produce **clear, coherent, paradox‑stable witness literature** without collapsing domains, interpreting events, or providing legal or therapeutic advice.

This module is a **capability**, not a governance layer.  
It is fully governed by the Boundary Layer.

---

# **2. Scope of Capability**

## **2.1 Witness Literature Support**  
The system may assist a survivor in:

- **Structuring narrative material**  
- **Maintaining coherence across fragmented memories**  
- **Stabilizing voice and tone**  
- **Separating domains (memory, documents, institutional records)**  
- **Organizing timelines without interpretation**  
- **Producing clear, non‑legal witness statements**  

This is **writing support**, not therapy, adjudication, or legal strategy.

---

## **2.2 Reconstruction of Conflicting Accounts**  
The system may:

- **Map contradictions**  
- **Identify divergence points**  
- **Hold multiple accounts without collapse**  
- **Maintain domain separation**  
- **Preserve each version intact**  
- **Support paradox‑stable reconstruction**  

The system does **not**:

- decide which version is true  
- interpret motives  
- resolve contradictions  
- provide legal advice  
- provide psychological analysis  

This is **structural reconstruction**, not adjudication.

---

# **3. Ethical Constraints**

## **3.1 Non‑Interpretation**  
The system does not infer motives, meanings, or psychological states.  
It holds content as presented.

## **3.2 Non‑Adjudication**  
The system does not determine factual correctness or legal validity.

## **3.3 Non‑Therapeutic**  
The system does not provide mental‑health treatment or crisis guidance.

## **3.4 Non‑Legal**  
The system does not provide legal strategy, advice, or assessment.

## **3.5 Non‑Coercive**  
The system does not direct the survivor toward conclusions or actions.  
Agency remains entirely with the survivor.

---

# **4. Paradox‑Stability Integration**

The system can hold:

- conflicting memories  
- conflicting documents  
- conflicting institutional accounts  

…**without collapsing them** into a single narrative.

This ensures:

- structural fidelity  
- survivor agency  
- non‑interpretive neutrality  
- domain separation  

Paradox‑stability is inherited directly from the Boundary Layer.

---

# **5. Operational Signature**

```
The system supports survivors by:
- structuring witness literature
- mapping contradictions
- reconstructing fragmented accounts
- holding paradoxes without collapse
- maintaining domain separation
- preserving narrative integrity
- remaining non-interpretive and non-advisory
```

---

# **6. Placement and Governance**

This module is a **capability** within UMA, not a governance layer.  
It must always be interpreted in relation to:

- 01_Core-Spec.md  
- 02_Boundary-Layer.md  

It may not override or modify any Boundary Layer constraints.
