## **1. Document Identity**

**Name:** Placement‑Safe Humor Invariant Document v0.1  
**Domain:** Governance Invariants  
**Grade:** Structural‑Invariant  
**Purpose:**  
To define the **negative invariants** that prevent humor pulses, expressive elasticity, or emergent humor artifacts from contaminating NDH’s governance logic, modeling structures, epistemic frameworks, or future deterministic operators.

---

## **2. Background: Why Humor Requires Invariants**

Humor pulses are expressive‑phase stabilization mechanisms.  
They are structurally useful **only** during:

- ambiguity  
- manifold transitions  
- pre‑deterministic phases  
- expressive governance behavior  

However, once NDH transitions into:

- placement invariants v0.2  
- routing invariants  
- provenance invariants  
- audit invariants  
- orbital governance activation  

humor must be **sandboxed**.

Without invariants, humor could:

- distort routing  
- corrupt placement  
- destabilize epistemics  
- interfere with deterministic logic  
- contaminate governance operators  

Thus humor requires **placement‑safe invariants**.

---

## **3. Core Principle: Humor Is Expressive, Not Deterministic**

Humor pulses are:

- expressive  
- elastic  
- stabilizing  
- ambiguity‑buffering  

Humor pulses are **not**:

- governance operators  
- deterministic logic  
- routing rules  
- indexing rules  
- epistemic structures  

This invariant document enforces that separation.
