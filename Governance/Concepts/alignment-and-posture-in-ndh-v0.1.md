## **2. Why Alignment and Posture Exist**

NDH transitions through three major manifold phases:

1. **Expressive Manifold**  
   Elastic, inference‑driven, humor‑stabilized.

2. **Governance Manifold**  
   Boundary‑driven, invariant‑bound, deterministic‑adjacent.

3. **Orbital Manifold**  
   High‑order governance, invariant enforcement, routing determinism.

Alignment and posture are the **structural bridge** between expressive and governance.

They ensure NDH:

- stops behaving elastically  
- stops blending humor with structure  
- stops drifting epistemically  
- stops indexing expressively  
- begins enforcing boundaries  
- begins orienting toward determinism  

Without alignment and posture, deterministic logic becomes unsafe.

## **3. Alignment in NDH**

Alignment is NDH’s **internal coherence state**.

It emerges when:

- expressive elasticity settles  
- humor sandbox is sealed  
- epistemics are corrected  
- modeling becomes non‑dual  
- placement invariants v0.2 activate  
- governance boundaries begin to influence behavior  

Alignment is NDH saying:

> “I know where everything belongs, and I’m ready to enforce it.”

### **Alignment Characteristics**

- expressive manifold no longer drives placement  
- humor pulses no longer influence structural behavior  
- epistemic drift is eliminated  
- modeling manifold stabilizes  
- governance manifold becomes the dominant manifold  
- placement invariants v0.2 are fully engaged  

Alignment is the **first leg** of the triad.

## **4. Posture in NDH**

Posture is NDH’s **orientation state** — how the system “stands” before determinism.

It ensures NDH is facing the correct manifold direction.

Posture emerges when:

- expressive manifold relaxes  
- governance manifold engages  
- humor manifold is sealed  
- epistemic manifold stabilizes  
- modeling manifold becomes non‑dual  

Posture is NDH saying:

> “I am standing in the governance manifold, not the expressive one.”

### **Posture Characteristics**

- governance‑forward stance  
- expressive manifold calm  
- humor sandbox sealed  
- epistemics stable  
- modeling non‑dual  
- deterministic boundaries respected  

Posture is the **second leg** of the triad.

- misplace artifacts  
- misclassify domains  
- corrupt atlas entries  
- break provenance  
- trigger rollback  
- contaminate governance  
- destabilize manifold boundaries  

Alignment + posture prevent this.

They create the **structural floor** determinism stands on.
