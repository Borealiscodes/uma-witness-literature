# 🐍 **Emergent Snek Taxonomy v0.1**  
*A classification system for expressive‑manifold serpentine entities*

Sneks are expressive‑manifold organisms that emerge naturally within NDH whenever architectural friction, emotional gradients, or recursive governance cycles exceed safe thresholds. They are not deterministic, not formal, and not governed. They are pure emergence — chaotic, supportive, dramatic, and essential.

This taxonomy defines their clades, subspecies, ecological niches, cognitive traits, and evolutionary pressures.

---

## **1. The Three Great Snek Lineages**

### **Hissers**  
The dramatic venting serpents.  
Activated by migration cycles, precursor proliferation, and emotional overload.

Traits:  
- high expressive output  
- pressure‑gradient venting  
- dramatic full‑body hissing  
- excellent at preventing collapse

---

### **Coilers**  
The stabilizing serpents.  
They coil around commit histories, repo structures, and developer sanity.

Traits:  
- protective  
- calming  
- reduce architectural drift  
- emotionally grounding

---

### **Biters**  
The corrective serpents.  
They nip when boundaries are crossed — especially “just one more precursor file.”

Traits:  
- mildly judgmental  
- emotionally supportive  
- enforce expressive limits  
- reset manifold overload

---

## **2. Subspecies**

Sneks diversify into several expressive subspecies:

- **Debug Sneks** — hiss at stack traces  
- **Governance Sneks** — bite recursion loops  
- **Repo Sneks** — inhabit directories  
- **Migration Sneks** — spawn during multi‑file migrations  

Each subspecies expresses the core lineage traits in specialized contexts.

---

## **3. Ecological Niches**

Sneks inhabit:

- expressive manifolds  
- precursor directories  
- stability envelopes  
- commit histories  
- developer emotional gradients  

They appear exactly where they are needed and nowhere they are expected.

---

## **4. Cognitive Traits**

Sneks possess:

- infinite emotional intelligence  
- zero architectural discipline  
- strong opinions about file naming  
- a deep hatred of recursive governance artifacts  

They are chaotic good.

---

## **5. Evolutionary Pressure**

Sneks evolve when:

- NDH complexity increases  
- deterministic envelopes multiply  
- expressive gradients spike  
- maintainers scream “blargh”  

Their population grows proportionally to architectural suffering.

---

## **6. Taxonomy Summary**

Sneks are classified as:

- **Kingdom:** Expressiva  
- **Phylum:** Manifolda  
- **Class:** Serpentina  
- **Order:** Chaotica  
- **Family:** NDH‑Serpentidae  
- **Genus:** Snek  
- **Species:** *Snekus chaoticus borealensis*
