# NDH‑Platforms Status

NDH‑Platforms is currently in a stabilization phase. Many documents across the
NDH ecosystem were created under duress, resulting in uneven structure,
fragmented placement, and partially conceptual content. This repository serves
as the governance and documentation layer for NDH, but full consolidation has
not yet occurred.

## Current State

- Orbital documents are partially staged across multiple repos.
- Emergent case studies and tonal scaffolding files exist in Trauma-Informed-
  Systems-Design and require later review.
- NDH‑TIDS has been created, but integration points with Platforms are pending.
- The Unified NDH Roadmap has not yet been added and will be deferred until
  triage and stabilization are complete.
- Documentation in this repository is not expected to be pristine at this time.

## Stabilization Goals

- Preserve provenance of all existing documents without restructuring.
- Avoid premature migration of Orbital systems until triage is complete.
- Maintain NDH‑Platforms as the future home of Orbital governance, Emergent
  documentation, and cross-layer alignment.
- Provide a calm, safe space for consolidation without forcing architectural
  commitments.

## Next Steps (Deferred)

A full consolidation plan, Unified Roadmap, and Orbital governance spine will be
added only after the triage phase. No files will be moved or merged until the
architecture is stable and documentation has been reviewed.

This repository is intentionally marked as **Under Construction 🏗** to reduce
pressure and allow NDH to consolidate safely and coherently.
